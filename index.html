Mahima,

There are people who walk into your life and rearrange the furniture — not loudly, not dramatically, but in the way sunlight does when it shifts through a window at 4 pm and suddenly everything looks softer than it did before.

You deserve every beautiful, soft, wonder-filled thing this year might bring.

With so much warmth, Krishna
